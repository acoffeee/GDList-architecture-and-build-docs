# GDList-architecture-and-build-docs
This will be the official documentation for GDList. GD list will be a database for geometry dash levels.

# About the project
we, the creators, are fond users of aniList, and MAL. These sites allow you to track your anime list. They provide comprensive features, some of which we would like to bring to geometry dash. The idea came when my friend showed me a spread sheet of some of their lists. but why should we have to use a spread sheet? and why cant they document additional information. Additionally, why is every list like the ccl or the csl or the challenge lists not available in easy to find places.
